How It Works...

  1. The Benefactor downloads the html file from github. Use Legacy's offline encryption protocol to create a key pair and encrypt your seed phrase.
  2. The Benefactor gives the Beneficiary Key to the Beneficiary to keep safe.
  3. The Benefactor Key and the Encrypted Seed Phrase are packed into a Deadman Switch whereby, upon the death of the Benefactor, an email is sent to the Beneficiary containing the Benefactor key and the Encrypted Seed Phrase. At this time, we do not offer this service and we recommend Gmail until something better becomes available.
  4. Finally, the Beneficiary enters both keys into the Legacy decrypt data fields and the seed phrase will be decrypted and ready to recover the Bitcoin.
