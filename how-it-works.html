How It Works...

  1. The Benefactor downloads the necessary files for Legacy's encryption system and uses it to generate a key pair and encrypt their seed phrase offline.
  2. One key is given to the Beneficiary to keep safe.
  3. The other key is kept by the Benefactor with the Encrypted Seed Phrase.
  4. The Benefactor sets up a Deadman Switch whereby, upon the death of the Benefactor, an email is sent to the Beneficiary.
  5. The email contains the Benefactor's key and the Encrypted Seed Phrase.
  6. Finally, the Beneficiary uses both keys to decrypt the seed phrase and recover the Bitcoin.
